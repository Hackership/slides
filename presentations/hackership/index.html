What's Hackership?

It's not a ship

well, not a physical one

think more of

Apprenticeships

but for coding

A Hackership is when ...

Hackers

of all fields, skills and strength come together

in the

distraction free

safe and open environment

of the dedicated

learning space

for three months full time

to learn from

and with each other

in hands-on

and result oriented style

with help of

industrial and learning experts

supporting them

to excel

and become

great

at what they do
